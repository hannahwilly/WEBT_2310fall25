Enjoy the changing leaves and be involved in being the change.

As the leaves change color and the air turns crisp, it's the perfect time to explore the great outdoors and enjoy seasonal festivities. Whether you're looking for scenic hikes, pumpkin patches, or local events, we've got you covered!

Community Service Opportunites and Giving Back

  • Community Cafe: A local eatery that donates a portion of its fall festival proceeds to support homeless shelters.
  • Harvest Helpers: A volunteer group that organizes fall clean-up events in local parks and nature reserves.
  • Autumn Arts Fair: An annual event where local artists showcase their work, with a percentage of sales going to community art programs.
